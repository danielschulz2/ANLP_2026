for when you want to run demo.py. The questions from the Step 4 Checkpoint are
hard-coded and they only make sense with the cat-facts.txt. But after the checkpoints
are done running you may ask questions that relate to your chosen txt.

*Instructions for running demo.py:* No extra instructions apart from choosing
the preferred txt-file at the top of the main function.
